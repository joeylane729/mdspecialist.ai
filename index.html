The specialist referral system in the U.S. is fragmented, inefficient, and a major source of patient frustration. Referrals are often based on insurance networks, doctor familiarity, and existing health networks rather than true expertise. This leads to suboptimal care, longer wait times, and billions in avoidable medical complications.
